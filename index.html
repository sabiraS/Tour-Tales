Reine, Lofoten Islands

Har du nytt ferien din i Norge?

Reine, in the Lofoten Islands, is renowned for its stunning red and white fishermen's cabins (rorbuer) set against dramatic mountain backdrops and clear blue waters. This picturesque village offers breathtaking views, outdoor activities like hiking and kayaking, and a rich cultural heritage. It's an ideal spot to witness the Northern Lights in winter and the midnight sun in summer.
